About us

Egg Bank Management & Consultancy was jointly founded by Chinese and German experts in reproductive medicine and embryo biology to provide consultancy and management services for egg bank projects with reference to the advanced technology and management experience of German reproductive medicine and egg freezing, preservation and transportation in order to promote the reserve and maintenance of fertility in China.

The company also provides a platform for Chinese fertility centres and their professionals to exchange with leading German fertility centres and research institutes and build bridges of cooperation.
