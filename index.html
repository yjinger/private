About us

Egg Bank Management & Consultancy was jointly founded by Chinese and German experts in reproductive medicine and embryo biology to provide consultancy and management services for fertility institutes with reference to the advanced technology and management experience of German reproductive medicine and egg freezing, preservation and transportation in order to promote the reserve and maintenance of fertility in China.

The company also provides a platform for Chinese fertility centres and their professionals to exchange with leading German fertility centres and research institutes and build bridges of cooperation.

Services

We provide fertility institutes with consultation and management services in oocyte freezing, transportation, storage, management and warming process.

We build a platform for exchange and discussion

Depending on the needs of the fertility institutes, Eggbank Management & Consultancy organises symposiums on different topics such as technical development and quality control for fertility institutes.

Company invites Chinese and German experts to visit and exchange with renowned fertility institutes in Germany, Switzerland and China, and also supports online seminars during special periods.

Training in embryology –
from basics to high-end.

Educational training is available for all relevant procedures in ART including scientific background information. Training modules can be arranged at your laboratory and with your own equipment.

  • Standard embryological laboratory techniques: OPU, IVF, ICSI, ET
  • PGS/PGD: polar body biopsy, blastocyst biopsy, FISH, array-CGH
  • Special laboratory techniques: laser in ART, polarization microscopy, time-lapse imaging Cryopreservation / Vitrification of oocytes and embryos
  • Fertility preservation: ovarian tissue freezing, cryobanking, transplantation
